North Carolina has implemented some precautions towards Covid-19. Some are listed below:

  • Reopening activities slowly to reduce spread
  • Requiring face coverings
  • Promoting the 3 W.s (Wear, wait, wash)
  • Statewide testing
  • Access to shelters for isolation

Canada has taken many great precautions towards Covid-19. Some are listed below:

  • Only using 4 airports
  • Screening officers
  • Quarantine officers
  • Clinical screening officers
  • Environmental Health officers
  • All travellers entering Canada must quarantine for 14 days
  • Canadian rangers deploy food and supplies into communities so citizens do not have to go out as much
  • Medical staff has been sent to long term care facilities
